# 🃏 台北金花

**An up-to-6-player betting game of incomplete information, aggression, and catastrophic reversals**

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Game

Win chips by **forcing opponents to fold** or **winning at showdown**. Eliminate opponents over the course of the tournament.

---

## 🪙 Section 1 — Setup

- **Players:** Up to 6
- **Deck:** Standard 52-card deck

**Each hand:**

- All players ante 1 unit
- Each player is dealt 3 cards face down

### 🏆 Tournament Structure

- Each player begins with **10,000 antes**
- A player who begins a hand with fewer than 2,000 antes is immediately eliminated — remaining chips are forfeited

### 💰 Stack Requirements

- **1,000 ante cap** — maximum bet size
- Players must have at least **2,000 antes** to be dealt in

---

## 🔄 Section 2 — Action Order

Play proceeds **clockwise**. First to act is the player to the left of the dealer.

**On your turn:**

- **Fold** — surrender your hand
- **Raise** — increase the bet
- **Close** — force immediate showdown

---

## 🃏 Section 3 — Hand Rankings (3-Card)

From highest to lowest:

| Rank | Hand |
|:----:|------|
| 1 | Prial (Three of a Kind) |
| 2 | Straight Flush |
| 3 | Straight |
| 4 | Flush |
| 5 | Pair |
| 6 | High Card |

> ### ⚠️ SPECIAL RULE — Low Beats Premiums
>
> At showdown: if at least one player has a **Flush or better** AND at least one player has a **qualifying low hand** — the lowest qualifying low wins the entire pot.

**Qualifying Low:**

- High card only (no pair, no straight, no flush)
- Highest card is **8 or lower**

**Clarifications:**

- Multiple qualifying lows → lowest hand wins
- No qualifying low → normal rankings apply
- No Flush or better → normal rankings apply

---

## 👁 Section 4 — Blind vs Seen

Players may look at their cards at any time. **Blind** = not looked. **Seen** = has looked.

| Action | Blind | Seen |
|--------|:-----:|:----:|
| Raise | 0.5× current bet | 1× current bet |
| Close | 1× current bet | 2× current bet |

---

## 💰 Section 5 — Betting Rules

- No-limit, up to the **1,000 ante cap**
- **Minimum opening bet:** 2 antes

**Raise Rules:** Each raise must exceed the current bet and increase by at least the previous increment.

> *Example: 5 → 10 (+5) → next raise must be ≥ 15*

---

## ⚡ Section 6 — Close (Force Showdown)

On your turn, instead of raising, you may **close** — immediately ending betting and forcing a showdown.

**Cost to Close:**

- Blind player: **1× current bet**
- Seen player: **2× current bet**

---

## 🔄 Section 7 — After a Close

Betting ends immediately. No further raises.

**🛡 Protected Player:** The player whose action was just closed pays nothing further and is automatically in the showdown.

Other players must either **pay in** or **fold**.

| Player Type | Cost to Stay In |
|-------------|:-----------------------:|
| Blind | 1× current bet |
| Seen | 2× current bet |

---

## 🃏 Section 8 — Showdown

All remaining (non-folded) players reveal their hands. Winner determined by hand rankings, subject to the Low Beats Premiums rule.

> ### 🔍 Full Reveal Rule (Edge Case)
>
> If any player reaches showdown with a qualifying low hand, **all hands are revealed** — including folded hands.
>
> However: folded hands are **never** eligible to win the pot.

**Resolution After Full Reveal:**

- Any revealed Flush or better → Low Beats Premiums triggered
- No such hand → normal rankings apply

# 🃏 台北金花

**最多6人的不完全訊息下注遊戲——充滿攻擊性與戲劇性逆轉**

---

## 🎯 遊戲目標

透過**迫使對手棄牌**或**在攤牌中獲勝**來贏取籌碼。在錦標賽過程中淘汰對手。

---

## 🪙 第一節——設置

- **玩家：** 最多6人
- **牌組：** 標準52張撲克牌

**每手牌：**

- 所有玩家下底注1單位
- 每位玩家發3張暗牌

### 🏆 錦標賽結構

- 每位玩家以 **10,000 底注**開始
- 若玩家在新手牌開始時少於 2,000 底注，則立即淘汰——剩餘籌碼沒收

### 💰 籌碼要求

- **1,000 底注上限**——最大下注額
- 玩家必須至少有 **2,000 底注**才能參與發牌

---

## 🔄 第二節——行動順序

按**順時針**進行。首位行動者為莊家左邊的玩家。

**輪到你時：**

- **棄牌（Fold）**——放棄手牌
- **加注（Raise）**——提高下注額
- **關閉（Close）**——強制立即攤牌

---

## 🃏 第三節——牌型排名（三張牌）

由高到低：

| 排名 | 牌型 |
|:----:|------|
| 1 | 豹子（三條 / Prial） |
| 2 | 同花順（Straight Flush） |
| 3 | 順子（Straight） |
| 4 | 同花（Flush） |
| 5 | 對子（Pair） |
| 6 | 散牌（High Card） |

> ### ⚠️ 特殊規則——小牌打優牌
>
> 攤牌時，若至少一位玩家有**同花或更高牌型**，且至少一位玩家有**符合條件的低牌**——最低的符合條件低牌贏得整個底池。

**符合條件的低牌：**

- 僅散牌（無對子、無順子、無同花）
- 最大牌為 **8 或更低**

**補充說明：**

- 多個符合條件的低牌 → 最低者勝
- 無符合條件的低牌 → 正常排名
- 無同花或更高牌型 → 正常排名

---

## 👁 第四節——暗牌 vs 明牌

玩家可隨時查看手牌。**暗牌** = 未看牌。**明牌** = 已看牌。

| 動作 | 暗牌 | 明牌 |
|------|:----:|:----:|
| 加注 | 0.5× 當前下注 | 1× 當前下注 |
| 關閉 | 1× 當前下注 | 2× 當前下注 |

---

## 💰 第五節——下注規則

- 無限制下注，上限為 **1,000 底注**
- **最低開注：** 2 底注

**加注規則：** 每次加注必須超過當前下注額，且增幅至少等於前一次的增幅。

> *範例：5 → 10（+5）→ 下次加注須 ≥ 15*

---

## ⚡ 第六節——關閉（強制攤牌）

輪到你時，你可以選擇**關閉**——立即結束下注並強制攤牌。

**關閉費用：**

- 暗牌玩家：**1× 當前下注**
- 明牌玩家：**2× 當前下注**

---

## 🔄 第七節——關閉之後

下注立即結束，不允許再加注。

**🛡 受保護玩家：** 被關閉行動的玩家無需再支付任何費用，自動進入攤牌。

其他玩家必須**付費留下**或**棄牌**。

| 玩家類型 | 留下費用 |
|----------|:--------------:|
| 暗牌 | 1× 當前下注 |
| 明牌 | 2× 當前下注 |

---

## 🃏 第八節——攤牌

所有未棄牌的玩家亮出手牌。根據牌型排名決定勝者（適用「小牌打優牌」規則）。

> ### 🔍 全部亮牌規則（邊緣情況）
>
> 若任何進入攤牌的玩家持有符合條件的低牌，**所有手牌均須亮出**——包括已棄牌的手牌。
>
> 但：已棄的手牌**永遠不能**贏得底池。

**全部亮牌後的裁決：**

- 若任何亮出的手牌為同花或更高牌型 → 觸發「小牌打優牌」
- 若無此類手牌 → 正常排名
